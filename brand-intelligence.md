# ARMADA Brand Intelligence

Official company sources:
- https://www.armada.net/
- https://www.armada.net/company/
- https://www.armada.net/solution/pro-technology/
- https://www.armada.net/armada-sunset-holdings-announces-strategic-integration/

Official logo source: https://www.armada.net/wp-content/themes/amd2024/public/images/logo-footer.3a20a9.svg
Official logo local path: `assets/brand/armada-logo-official.svg`
A user-supplied logo reference was used only for initial visual comparison; the published campaign uses the official SVG retrieved by the deployment workflow.

Visible company identity: the unmodified ARMADA logo appears above the fold and on document headers, paired with independent candidate wording.

Color token provenance:
- Source-sampled from supplied logo: blue `#0866c4`, sky `#49a6df`, violet `#6155e8`, lime `#9cde1c`, teal overlap `#0d665c`, charcoal `#4a4a4d`.
- Candidate-original role colors: deep navy `#071f39` and secondary navy `#0b2e4c`.

Typography evidence and decision:
- Public ARMADA presentation uses bold geometric/humanist sans-serif headlines and clean utility text.
- The logo wordmark is a wide geometric sans.
- Licensed/system substitute: Cabin for body and Clear Sans for display hierarchy. No proprietary font files are copied or committed.

Photography/illustration character:
- Official pages use bold full-width supply-chain imagery and high-contrast technology visuals.
- This campaign uses original code-native routing-table graphics rather than external photography to avoid implying ownership of ARMADA facilities or products.

Geometry and interface motifs:
- Directional triangular energy from the supplied logo.
- Blue/sky/violet/lime recognition layer.
- Open, high-contrast typography and operating-system diagrams.

Motion character:
- A request signal moves through a complete outcome-evidence-delivery-adoption path.
- Scenario and slider changes alter the disposition and active gates.
- Reduced-motion mode removes continuous motion while retaining all states and controls.

Overall vibe: precise, energetic, operational, transparent, and optimistic.

Original visuals created:
- Outcome Routing Table
- Build / Validate / Sequence / Stop disposition system
- Role-specific scorecard and evidence trail

Recommended use by artifact:
- Website: full brand palette and routing interaction.
- Resume / cover letter: restrained blue rules, logo, clean document geometry.
- Brief / plan / worksheet: shared header and role-specific routing terminology.

Independent-candidate distinction: every public surface identifies the work as Russell Dudek's independent candidate vision or candidate materials and includes a non-endorsement statement where appropriate.
