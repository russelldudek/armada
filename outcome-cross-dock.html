Decision productWhat recurring business decision or behavior will this report, dataset, automation, or AI-assisted solution improve?
Accountable ownerWho owns the decision and adoption—not merely the request?
Value and urgencyWhat credible consequence exists today? What makes this more important than competing work?
ReadinessWhat is known about sources, definitions, lineage, quality, access, dependencies, and technical feasibility?
DispositionBuild now, shape next, explore, or stop/hold? Why?
Release contractWhat must be true for quality, validation, access, usability, support, failure handling, security/governance, and human authority?
Adoption mechanismWhere will the product enter the workflow? What behavior changes? Who reinforces it?
Outcome evidenceWhat will be observed after release—use, decision behavior, cycle time, rework, quality, support, exceptions, or another relevant measure?
Learning dispositionScale, standardize, reuse, revise, support differently, preserve as exception, hold, or retire?
Review test: if the team cannot name the decision owner, release conditions, and evidence after launch, the item is not ready to be treated as a committed data product.

Illustrative candidate artifact. It does not represent ARMADA’s current process or internal data.
