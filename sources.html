Brand asset provenance

The campaign uses ARMADA’s official footer SVG for nominative identification. The publication workflow retrieves https://www.armada.net/wp-content/themes/amd2024/public/images/logo-footer.3a20a9.svg directly from ARMADA’s current website and commits it locally under assets/brand/. The mark remains unmodified and is displayed on a restrained navy field for legibility. Colors are source-sampled from ARMADA’s public identity and labeled as such. Lato is used as a licensed structural substitute for ARMADA’s bold, clean public sans-serif character; no proprietary font files are copied.

Candidate evidence boundary

Career facts, titles, dates, metrics, credentials, and toolkit claims are limited to verified candidate evidence. Power BI and Microsoft Fabric are treated as a role-specific ramp area; no production-estate ownership or unverified platform tenure is claimed.

Hypotheses for discovery

  • The 2026 enterprise integration may increase demand for shared definitions, visibility, and reusable internal data products.
  • A lightweight portfolio and adoption layer may reduce escalation, surprise, and rework.
  • The strongest first proof may be one bounded Power BI/Fabric workstream with a real decision owner and observable adoption path.
